Our Story

Through adversity and fate Floor Artist Crew (FAC) was established in 2010. It was originally created by Phia, Shernou, King and Dream. FAC member are consist of great indiviaul with different artistry and finesse. FAC is renown for winning many Local, Bay Area and Midwest events. As a Family we are finally coming together to start our own events, teach classes and suport the community. We want to help Bboys and Bgirls all over the world to learn the best way, have the best gear and get the support they need. Together we can be Olympic ready!
